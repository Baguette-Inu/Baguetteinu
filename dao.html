🔹 What is the Baguette Inu DAO?

The Baguette Inu DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) is a community-driven governance system that gives every $BGI holder the power to vote on key decisions regarding the project’s future.

  • 💡 Community-Powered: Every decision is made by the holders of $BGI.
  • 💡 Fair & Transparent: All transactions and votes are recorded on the blockchain.
  • 💡 Decentralized: No single person or entity controls the ecosystem.
  • 💡 Sustainable Growth: The DAO ensures long-term governance beyond individual leadership.

📜 How Does the DAO Work?

1️⃣ Proposal Submission:

  • ✅ Any community member holding a minimum amount of $BGI can submit proposals.
  • ✅ Proposals must be related to game updates, marketing strategies, partnerships, festival locations, staking rewards, token burns, or NFT sales.

2️⃣ Community Voting:

  • ✅ Proposals are presented to all $BGI holders.
  • ✅ Voting is conducted through smart contracts where token holders can vote based on the number of tokens they hold (weighted voting).
  • ✅ Once voting closes, the proposal is either approved or rejected.

3️⃣ Execution:

  • ✅ If the proposal is approved, the DAO automatically executes the smart contract or delegates the decision to the core development team.
  • ✅ The treasury wallet releases funds for development, marketing, events, and community incentives.

🌍 What Can the Community Vote On?

  • 🔹 Mini-Game Evolution: Choose new game features, mechanics, and rewards.
  • 🔹 Next Festival Location: The community decides which city hosts the next Baguette Inu Festival.
  • 🔹 Marketing Strategy: Vote on influencer partnerships, ad campaigns, and collaborations.
  • 🔹 Tokenomics Adjustments: Decide on staking rates, burning events, and liquidity pool allocations.
  • 🔹 NFT Development: Choose the next collection themes, utilities, and reward structures.
  • 🔹 Treasury Management: Approve how the DAO funds are spent, ensuring transparency.

💰 DAO Treasury – Transparent Fund Management

💼 The DAO manages a publicly visible treasury wallet where funds from:

  • ✅ NFT sales, staking rewards, mini-game transactions, and festival earnings are stored.
  • ✅ A real-time dashboard tracks all incoming and outgoing funds.

📊 Tracking in Real-Time:

  • 📌 DAO Treasury wallet balance displayed live on the website.
  • 📌 Transactions are automatically updated on a public Solscan dashboard.
  • 📌 A Telegram Bot sends real-time notifications of fund movements.

🚀 How to Join the DAO & Participate?

  • ✅ Step 1: Hold $BGI Tokens in a compatible wallet (Phantom, Solflare).
  • ✅ Step 2: Connect your wallet to the DAO platform via the website.
  • ✅ Step 3: Browse proposals and participate in discussions on Discord/Telegram.
  • ✅ Step 4: Cast your vote directly through the blockchain interface.
  • ✅ Step 5: Track proposal results and treasury updates in real-time.
