Before you can grab your crispy $BGI, you’ll need SOL, the native currency of Solana.

🔹 Step 2: Transfer SOL to Your Wallet 📤

Once you’ve purchased SOL, transfer it to your non-custodial Solana wallet.

💡 Jupiter ensures the best swap rates, so you get the most $BGI for your SOL!

Now, let’s convert your SOL into $BGI:
